
## Dyson V12 Repair Project

**Model:** Dyson V12 Detect Slim
**Problem:** Random shutoffs — known capacitor failure issue

**Parts ordered:**
- 270uF 35V capacitors (Witonics 10-pack)
- TOWOT desoldering kit (wick + wire + sucker)

**Reference threads:**
- iFixit: https://www.ifixit.com/Answers/View/869148
- Reddit: https://www.reddit.com/r/dyson/comments/1k2gkzj/i_just_repaired_my_v12_detect_slim_turning_off/

**Repair steps:**
1. Open the body (Torx screws)
2. Find the 3 capacitors on the main board
3. Note polarity (+ and - orientation!)
4. Desolder the old caps
5. Solder in the new 270uF 35V caps
6. Reassemble
7. Test
