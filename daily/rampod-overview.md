# RAMPOD Program Overview
*Source: RAMPOD Demo Presentation (Feb 19, 2026)*

## Mission Statement
RAMPOD = **Reliability, Availability, and Maintainability Logistics Engineering Support System for Pods**

Provides serialized asset tracking to collect, process, collate, validate, report, and archive reliability, availability, maintainability, configuration, inventory, status, performance (sortie/mission), maintenance and warranty data for Pods and Integrated Systems through web-based applications.

**Accountable Property System of Record (APSR) for AF Pods.**

## Scale
- **2,400+ users** (AF, Army, Navy, DoD & Contractors)
- **114 CONUS + 12 OCONUS sites**
- **5,000+ AF pods** with Total Acquisition Cost **exceeding $7.2B**
- **6 automated interfaces** for receipt/transfer of maintenance data with other systems
- Users include: USAF, ANG, AFRES, Navy, Army

## Primary Functional Areas
- Asset Management (Inventory, Status, Configuration)
- Maintenance Data
- Metrics (user-defined, engineering data for cause/effect)
- Historical Reports
- Real-time pod/equipment status and location
- Serialized tracking of replaced LRUs & SRUs
- Interface to IMDS (reduces dual entry, improves accuracy)

## Applications Suite
1. **Global Eye (GE)** — Asset tracking for avionics systems and SE for A-10, B-1B, B-2, F-15, F-16
2. **PARS** (Pod Asset and Reporting System) — Enterprise-level pod inventory tracking + SE visibility
3. **HTS** (HARM Targeting System) — HTS pod failure reporting
4. **ACTS** (Pod Collection Software/Air Combat Test and Training System) — ACTS failure reporting to PMO
5. **CRIIS** (Common Range Integrated Instrumentation System) — Inventory/status of multi-service combat simulation pods
6. **RIMSS** (RAMPOD Inventory & Maintenance System Software) — Used by HQ ACC and pod Program Offices for program management
7. **RCS** (RAMPOD Collection System) — Maintenance history collection for EW pods and test equipment
