### The App: CashPilot
- **Concept:** Budget app where you TALK to your money. AI agent does CRUD, not just Q&A.
- **Niche:** ADHD-friendly + Freelancer-ready + AI-agent-powered + Affordable ($4.99/mo)
- **Stack:** Swift/SwiftUI/SwiftData, Vercel serverless, OpenRouter → Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Competitor research:** Nova analyzed 13 competitors (YNAB, Monarch, Copilot, etc.)
- **Nobody occupies this intersection.** That's the niche Justin "created out of thin air."

### What We Built (in order)
1. **Phase 1:** Foundation — 8 SwiftData models, DataManager, ProManager, KeyboardManager, Theme, 4-tab layout, iCloud (deferred)
2. **Phase 2:** The Agent — AgentService with 12 function-calling tools, ChatView (iMessage-style), Vercel API route with full system prompt, MemoryManager, ContextBuilder, ResponseParser ([TAG] system)
3. **Phase 3:** Dashboard (Free-to-Spend hero, donut chart, progress bars), BudgetView, TransactionListView, CategoryDetailView, MoreView
4. **Phase 4:** Bills, Income Sources, Savings Goals, Achievements (15 badges), Onboarding (3-screen carousel), Paywall (full Apple 3.1.2c boilerplate)
5. **Phase 5:** Voice input (SpeechRecognizer + VoiceInputButton), Photo Import (Gemini Vision OCR), ReviewService, NotificationManager
